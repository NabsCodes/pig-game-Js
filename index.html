Pig Game Rules 🚩

📍 Objective:

- Reach 100 points before your opponent.

🎲 Gameplay:

- Roll the dice to add points to your current score.

- If you roll a 1, you lose your current score.

- Click "Hold" to add your current score to your total.

🏆 Winning:

- First to 100 points wins

🆕 New Game:

- Resets scores for a fresh game.
